Methods of supply

-          Local purchase order (LPO)

-          Government acquisition card

-          Contract

-          Contract with task authorization (TA)

-          Standing offer (SO)

-          Supply arrangement (SA)

All methods of supply for federal procurement may be employed under modern treaties/CLCAs, provided the obligations of the applicable modern treaty/CLCA have been addressed.

For request for standing offers (RFSO), request for supply arrangements (RFSA) and contracts with task authorization (TA), the following instrument options can be established: 

  • An instrument that provides for both modern treaty areas and non-modern treaty areas;
  • Two streams of instruments - one for modern treaty areas and one for non-modern treaty areas; or
  • Multiple streams for various geographic or modern treaty areas.

Read through the existing mandatory SOs or SAs. If they do not fulfill the obligations of the applicable modern treaty (CLCA), you are exempted from having to use them. Establish a separate contract, SO or SA, that addresses your requirement.
