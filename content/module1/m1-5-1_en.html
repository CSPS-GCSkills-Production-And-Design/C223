2.6.2.      PSAB

Stimulating Indigenous economic development using the PSAB set-asides

If no compliant bids were received when the modern treaty/CLCA provided the beneficiary businesses with a right of first refusal, consider setting the procurement aside under the Procurement Strategy for Aboriginal Business (PSAB).

The obligations of the applicable modern treaty/CLCA always take precedence

As long as the obligations of any specific modern treaty/CLCA and associated IBAs such as the Park Co-management Agreement are first complied with, the PSABâ€™s set-asides can be invoked to meet procurement requirements.

Seek guidance

If no right of first refusal exists under the modern treaty/CLCA, and setting aside the procurement under the PSAB is not practical due to the specific requirements of the procurement, consult your departmental Indigenous procurement team or ISC to identify other ways to ensure the procurement benefits modern treaty/CLCA communities.
