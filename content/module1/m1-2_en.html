2.3.            CLCAs matter

There are many reasons why modern treaties/CLCAs are important.

You have a legal duty to fulfill the obligations of the modern treaties/CLCAs.

Treaties are constitutionally protected, mutually binding agreements. The rights defined in them are protected within the Constitution Act. They apply regardless of dollar value, and apply to both competitive and non-competitive procurement. Failing to do so may result in costly litigation for the Crown.

Your role and contribution is critical toward the goal of reconciliation.

Modern treaties/CLCAs establish ongoing and evolving relationships in which reconciliation is an important aim. The way you address procurement obligations can advance reconciliation with Indigenous people in Canada.
