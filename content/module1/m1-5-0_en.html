2.6.1.      Key considerations

The requirements have been refined to take modern treaty/CLCA obligations into account. It is now your time to develop the procurement strategy and ensure that key decisions such as selecting the sourcing method, determining the method of supply and establishing the procurement schedule continues to address applicable modern treaty/CLCA obligations. 

But here’s the truth…

There is no “one-size fits all” approach to developing a procurement strategy that satisfies the client’s operational requirements, achieves best value and meets modern treaty/CLCA obligations. Depending on the complexity of your project, the strategy could be quite straightforward, or it could be more detailed and require significant critical thinking, problem-solving and decision-making. 

What can you expect from this section

To ensure your decisions are informed and in accordance with modern treaty/CLCA obligations, this section will address the key considerations you should be aware of when developing the procurement strategy.
