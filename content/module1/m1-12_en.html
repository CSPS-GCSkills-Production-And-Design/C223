2.13. File documentation

Detailed and accurate documentation of the procurement file is critical to protecting Canada in the event there is a need to confirm the proper steps were taken while procuring in modern treaty/CLCA areas. Procurement plans or approval documents are methods of documenting process and decisions on Indigenous procurements.
