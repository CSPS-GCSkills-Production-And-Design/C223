4.7. Contract monitoring

As with any contract, active and ongoing monitoring and documenting of contractor performance can help avoid or mitigate contract risks and facilitate their successful resolution. 

When modern treaties/CLCAs are applicable, ensure that commitments are achieved in accordance with contractual obligations.
