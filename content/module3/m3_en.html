2.9. Summary

Congratulations on completing module 1. 

In summary:

  • Use the final delivery point or the location of the performance of the good, service and construction to determine if it is covered by any modern treaties/CLCAs.
  • Contact your departmental Indigenous procurement team or Indigenous Services Canada (ISC) to determine the applicability of a modern treaty/CLCA and to identify, understand and fulfill applicable procurement obligations.
  • If there is any doubt, contact Indigenous Services Canada (ISC) or your departmental Indigenous procurement teams.
