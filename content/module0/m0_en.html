1. Learning objectives

Gain a broader understanding of Comprehensive Land Claims Agreements (CLCAs), often referred to as modern treaties, and how their obligations affect procurement activities.

At the end of this module, you will be able to:

- Determine if a procurement is subject to any modern treaty/CLCA.

- Describe how to identify applicable modern treaty/CLCA obligations.

- Summarize the most common procurement obligations found in modern treaties/CLCAs.

- Explain how modern treaties/CLCAs might affect certain steps of the procurement process.
