Contracting authority

You work with clients to plan and execute the acquisition of goods, services and construction services in a manner that supports the delivery of government programs while providing the best value for the Crown.

Also known as: contracting officer, procurement officer, buyer and procurement specialist.


Business owner

A business owner offers operational and technical expertise while collaborating with the contracting authority, including planning procurements that leverage socio-economic goals.

Also known as: client, responsibility centre manager, technical/scientific authority, functional authority, project manager/authority/sponsor.


Acquisition card holder

You have the delegated authority under section 32 of your departmental delegation of authority instrument and have been issued a GoC acquisition card for government business.


Policy analyst

You develop and manage policies by which organizations are able to operate fairly, consistently and efficiently.
